CHAPTER 1: INTRODUCTION TO THE PROJECT TOPIC

Recruitment is a process of attracting a pool of potential candidates from the labour market to apply for a job, whereas selection is an elimination process to choose the best fit for the job. Recruitment is the process of searching for and attracting qualified candidates to apply for the available positions.
